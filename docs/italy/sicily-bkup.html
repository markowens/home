Trip Overview

Tour the old city of Palermo with its incredible mix of architectural styles, discover Agrigento’s Valley of the Temples, ascend the slopes of Mt. Etna, visit the Norman Cathedral in Monreale with its glittering Byzantine mosaics, explore the Greek Theater in Taormina, and admire the Roman Villa del Casale.

Enjoy two days in Sorrento, drive the spectacular Amalfi Coast, tour Pompeii, spend time in Rome, and optionally visit Florence.
